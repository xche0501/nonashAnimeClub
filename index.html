Our Story

About The Club

The Nonash Anime Club was founded by passionate university students who wanted to create a welcoming community for anime and manga fans on campus. What started as a small group watching episodes in a lecture hall has grown into one of the most active clubs at our university.

We believe in bringing people together through a shared love of Japanese animations, comics, and culture. Whether you are a hardcore otaku or just getting started, there is a place for you here.

Weekly Screenings

Join us for weekly anime screenings. We watch everything from popular seasonal shows to all-time classics on the big screen with your fellow club members!
